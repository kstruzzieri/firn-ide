Looking at your momentum-lstm-v3 config, the walk-forward validation shows a 0.4 Sharpe drop in the out-of-sample period. This is a classic sign of overfitting.

The issue: Your LSTM has 3 layers with 128 units each, but you only have 8,760 training samples. That's too much model capacity.

Try adding dropout between layers:
